*Compliance With Wisconsin Alcohol Law:*

IT IS WITH UNDERSTANDING THAT THE ATTENDEE MAKES FULL EFFORT TO UNDERSTAND THE LOCAL, FEDERAL, and STATE LAWS THAT ARE MENTIONED AND OR APPLICABLE TO THIS WAIVER.

1. Attendees must comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws regarding alcohol.

3. Under **Wisconsin Statute §125.035**, Wisconsin provides certain limitations on civil liability related to alcohol consumption; however, those protections do not apply when alcohol is provided to underage individuals.
